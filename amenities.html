Amenities & Services

What's Included

Everything You Need

Our amenities are designed to make your stay effortless and enjoyable — from arrival to departure.

🍳

Complimentary Breakfast

Enjoy a full continental breakfast each morning — eggs, fresh pastries, fruit, yogurt, and hot coffee. Served 6:30–10:00 AM daily.

🏊

Outdoor Pool

Our sparkling outdoor pool is open seasonally (May–September), with lounge chairs and towel service available for all guests.

📶

Free High-Speed Wi-Fi

Stay connected throughout the property with complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi — no password required in public areas.

🅿️

Free On-Site Parking

Ample complimentary parking for all registered guests, including accessible spaces near the main entrance.

💪

Fitness Center

Stay active in our fully-equipped fitness center featuring cardio machines, free weights, and stretching mats. Open 24 hours.

🧺

Laundry Facilities

Self-service coin laundry is available on-site. Dry-cleaning referrals can be arranged through the front desk.

☕

24-Hour Coffee Station

Fresh coffee, tea, and hot cocoa available around the clock in our lobby lounge area.

🛎️

Concierge Services

Our friendly front desk team is available 24/7 to assist with reservations, local recommendations, and transportation arrangements.

♿

Accessible Facilities

ADA-compliant rooms, accessible parking, ramps, and ground-floor accommodations ensure a comfortable stay for every guest.

🐾

Pet-Friendly Rooms

Travelling with a furry friend? We welcome well-behaved pets in select rooms. A $25/night pet fee applies. Contact us to reserve.

📺

Smart TVs

Every room features a 55″ Smart TV with streaming apps pre-loaded — Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, and more.

🌿

Garden Courtyard

Relax in our beautifully landscaped courtyard — a peaceful outdoor space with seating, ideal for morning coffee or evening unwinding.
